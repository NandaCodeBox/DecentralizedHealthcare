## Accessibility Guidelines

1. **Always include alt text** for screen readers
2. **Use semantic HTML** with proper ARIA labels
3. **Ensure sufficient color contrast** (WCAG AA minimum)
4. **Provide text labels** alongside icons
5. **Test with screen readers** (NVDA, JAWS)
6. **Use consistent icon sizes** across the app

---

## Icon Best Practices
