   - General practice
   - Extended hours
   - Consultation services

3. PHARMACIES (Self-Care)
   - MediCare Pharmacy
   - Medicines & supplies
   - 24/7 availability
   - Delivery services

4. DIAGNOSTIC CENTERS (Routine Care)
   - Wellness Diagnostic Center
   - Lab tests & imaging
   - Reports available

Each facility shows:

- Star rating and reviews
- Operating hours/availability
- Key services offered
- "Get Care" button for easy access
